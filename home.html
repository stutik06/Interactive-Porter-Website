FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is porter app?

Porter is an app based mini truck and bike booking service. Just log in, choose from a number of mini-truck options or 2-wheeler and book! Get a verified driver and vehicle with an ETA of under 15 minutes.

How do I use Porter App?

  1. Download Porter App
  2. Select pick up and delivery destination.
  3. Choose and rent from 2 wheeler and various mini trucks options (Tata Ace, Pickup 8ft, Pickup 7ft, Tempo etc)
  4. Vehicle arrives in under 15 minutes

How do I book a tempo/mini truck or bike online from porter?

Porter is an app that provides affordable and reliable moving services. Steps to hire a vehicle:
  1. Sign into the Porter app.
  2. Browse through the list of available vehicles (Tata Ace, Pickup 8ft, 2 wheeler etc) and pick the one that suits your moving needs.
  3. Set your pick up and drop off location.
  4. Enter a few additional details, apply coupons to get exciting discounts, and confirm your booking.
  5. Sit back and relax- our verified driver partners and technology will do the rest.

Does porter provide intercity or interstate service?

Yes, Porter provides intercity & interstate services as well. The intercity & interstate service allows you to deliver goods anywhere within 300 km radius from the city center.

Does porter provide labour (loading/unloading) service?

Yes, Porter has lauched a new category called the "Driver-Helper" category, where the Driver-partner will help in loading and unloading facility. Our Driver-Partner will assist you in loading and unloading goods from the ground to the ground floor.

What are charges for a porter truck and porter bike delivery service?

The charges for hiring a porter truck and porter bike are based on the number of kilometres travelled, type of vehicle and differs from locality to locality. You can also check the estimate tab on the Porter page to get an exact idea of your trip amount.

Does Porter provide Packers and Movers services?

We have launched Packers and Movers services in Bangalore, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Pune and Chennai, and will be launching soon in other cities. To avail or enquire about this, call 4410 4410 (add your city code).

Does Porter provide mini trucks like tata ace on monthly contract?

No, we do not provide mini trucks on fixed monthly contract. For business solutions by Porter, visit Porter for Enterprise page..

Does Porter deliver laptop?

Yes, we happily deliver laptop, documents, gadgets, lunch boxes, forgotten keys, etc via Porter's two wheeler service.

How long does it take to deliver?

The distance & traffic play a major role in determining the delivery time, but we guarantee same day delivery.
